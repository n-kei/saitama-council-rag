Carousel displays a list of widgets in a slideshow format with navigation buttons.
Carousels are available for Google Workspace add-ons and Google Chat apps.
The
addCarouselCard(card)
method is used to add a card to the carousel.
Carousel
Carousel, also known as slider, rotates and displays a list of widgets in a slideshow format,
with buttons navigating to the previous or next widget.
Available for Google Workspace add-ons and Google Chat apps.
